About Us

WHO: We're a family owned butcher shop in San Francisco's Marina District serving up meat, poultry, seafood and everything else you'll need.

WHAT/WHY: We source humanely raised meat and support small farms. We believe in whole animal utilization and sustainable farming practices. We get whole carcass animals in and break them down into different cuts (MAYBE LINK).
